About P2P Transport

Building trust through reliable transportation since 1998

Our Story

Founded in 1998, P2P Transport began as a small family-owned trucking company with just two trucks and a dream to provide reliable transportation services. Over the past 25+ years, we've grown into one of the region's most trusted logistics partners.

Our commitment to excellence, safety, and customer satisfaction has been the driving force behind our success. We've expanded our fleet to over 500 vehicles and established partnerships with thousands of businesses across the United States.

Today, P2P Transport continues to uphold the values that founded our company: integrity, reliability, and exceptional service. We're not just moving cargo; we're moving America forward.

Our Mission

To provide safe, reliable, and efficient transportation solutions that exceed our customers' expectations while contributing to the economic growth of the communities we serve.

Our Vision

To be the leading transportation and logistics company, recognized for our innovation, sustainability, and unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction.

Our Values

Safety first, integrity in all dealings, excellence in service delivery, respect for our team and customers, and environmental responsibility.
